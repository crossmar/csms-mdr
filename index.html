About CSMS-MDR

CSMS-MDR is an easy-to-explore database of ship-based measurements that are taken during monthly cruises to the THEMO observatory, as part of the ongoing research activity at the Haifa University Charney School of Marine Sciences ongoing research activity.


Guided by the recognition that sharing data and knowledge has a dramatic contribution to our ability to understand environmental process, all data that will be collected in the course of this project will be publicly available, and efforts will be made to make the data them accessible and easy to use.
